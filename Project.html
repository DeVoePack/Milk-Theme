sorry to bother you since i'm sure your busy but why did you abandon welsh and shedar? It honestly looked quite promising but i'm just curious and follow up question is there any chance at all that you will ever bring it back?

Hey there, Anon! Let me start with saying that I (wishingstarinajar) have no hand in what was ’Dofus: Welsh & Shedar’. This canceled/abandoned project belongs to Ankama, the studio responsible for anything Dofus, Wakfu and related, be it the games, comics or animated series.

Ankama cancelled the series during production because it wasn’t what the TV channel they were working with (France 3) were looking for; France 3 wanted something more lighthearted and less focused on a heavy plot to appease a young audience. A few parts and aesthetics of ‘Dofus: Welsh & Shedar’ were rehashed into a different series called ‘Dofus: Aux Trésors de Kerubim’ (2012) and the movie ‘Dofus Livre 1: Julith’ (2016), which is still available in selected French cinemas.

This said, Anthony Roux (Tot), producer and founder of Ankama, has hinted on a revival of ‘Dofus: Welsh & Shedar’ IF there is enough interest for it. We’ll have to wait and see!
